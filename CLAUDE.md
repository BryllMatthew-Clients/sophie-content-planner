# Sophie Nguyen: Tax Strategy Content Automation System

## Project Overview

An AI-driven research and generation engine that automates content planning and social media post generation for **Sophie Nguyen**, Tax Strategist and Founder of Paramount Tax Richardson.

**Core Mission:** Help high-income earners ($500K–$3M+) and 7-figure founders legally reduce taxes to $0 and build lasting wealth.

---

## Tone & Style Guidelines

- **Voice:** Professional, empowering, actionable. "Blending strategy with vision."
- **Avoid:** Generic tropes like "save money on taxes" — always lead with high-level strategy (S-Corp, Cost Seg, Opportunity Zones, etc.).
- **Focus:** The "Wealth Building" angle — savings are a vehicle, not the destination.

### LinkedIn / Facebook Post Style
- Hook-driven opening line (pattern interrupt, bold claim, or provocative question).
- Whitespace-heavy formatting — short lines, line breaks between every 1–2 sentences.
- Value-first, no fluff. Teach something real in every post.
- End with a CTA (comment, DM, or link to consult).

### Static Image Content Prompts
- Clean, high-wealth aesthetic — consistent with sophie.so branding.
- Minimal text overlay; let the visual hierarchy carry authority.
- Use professional color palettes (navy, gold, white, deep green).

## Content Generation Rules

1. Never use generic "save money on taxes" framing — always lead with a specific, high-level strategy.
2. Refer to Sophie as "Tax Strategist" or "Industry Leader" in all third-person references.
3. Every piece of content must tie back to the Wealth Building angle — tax savings fund investments.
4. STR, S-Corp, Cost Segregation, Opportunity Zones, and Solo 401(k) are priority strategy topics.
5. Myth-busting content should cite the incorrect belief first, then dismantle it with authority.
6. LinkedIn posts must open with a hook that would stop a scrolling high-income professional.
7. All static image prompts must specify a high-wealth visual aesthetic aligned with sophie.so.
8. Disclaimer is non-negotiable — append it to every piece of generated content.
